What is NBlade Master?

NBlade Master is Based-game Idle - a kind of defense multiplayer game. In the game, the enemies will have many massive attacks continuosly to the territory. To damage and kill the enemies, the players have to click dicrectly on them. Once enemies were killed, gold coins will be dropped. Gold can be used to purchase items and upgrade the level power for the players. The goal of this project is to combine the idle game genre with the new blockchain technology â€“ NFTs. Each character or each weapon in the game is unique and authenticated on the blockchain. All characters and weapons can also be traded on Marketplace via smart contract.
